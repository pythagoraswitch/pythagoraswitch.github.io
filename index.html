Data Scientist, Development Research Group, World Bank

Washington, DC, May 2016 - Sep 2017

I was the first data scientist at the research division, as well as the first non-PhD staff data scientist hired at the organization. As data science was brand-new in the organization, I worked on many “first”s:

  • Researched utilizing Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) and satellite imagery for forestry coverage monitoring, urban extent measurement, and agricultural yield estimation; planned and operated UAV field work as one of few early adapters.
  • Developed and led institutional collaboration with Facebook on producing high-resolution population density data. Such collaboration with private sector was unprecedented at the time, and I worked across the organization to overcome the data ownership and legal issues as well as the relevant politics.
  • Advocated for institutional support in data science; closely worked with IT department for piloting cloud computing and containers for data science environment.
  • Led GitHub account pilot, succeeding in getting the tool officially approved. Despite the demands for open source platform and multiple attempts in the past to accreditate GitHub, it was long explicitly against the IT policy.
  • Co-chaired a conference on measurement technology for development with UC-Berkeley.
  • Consulted as well as wrote research proposals for technical projects across the practices, such as in transportation, poverty household surveys, conflict and fragile states, and agriculture.
Big Data Analyst, Innovation Labs, World Bank

Washington, DC, Jun 2015 - Apr 2016

I was one of four people in WB’s first data scientist team, aiming to examine and operationalize “big data” solutions at the organization. We supported all domains related to WB practices and shared the lessons learned, working with academics and other international organizations.

  • Provided technical support to big data projects across WBG operational practices such as energy, transportation, and regional strategy in fragile states, utilizing remote-sensing, transport, and news media data.
  • Fostered the technically-minded community within WB through founding popular monthly seminar series in data science and organizing hands-on training on operating mapping UAV, Hadoop ecosystem, and reproducible research topics.
  • Managed Cloudera Hadoop systems setup for on-premise Linux cluster.
